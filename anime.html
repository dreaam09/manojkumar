Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba Infinity Castle First Movie Releases New Visual

Following the reveal at the Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba Infinity Castle panel at San Diego Comic-Con todayOpens in a new tab, the official website and accounts for the series released a new visual featuring Tanjiro and Giyu versus Akaza. The tagline reads “a place where only the strong stand.”

Tanjiro and Giyu Tomioka versus Akaza

The first major fight in the Infinity Castle arc of Demon Slayer is Tanjiro and Giyu Tomioka versus Akaza, the Upper Rank Three demon. This confrontation takes place as the Demon Slayers infiltrate the Infinity Castle to confront Muzan Kibutsuji. Tanjiro, seeking to avenge Rengoku's death, and Giyu, testing his strength, engage Akaza in a battle that reveals Akaza's backstory and sees Tanjiro awaken the "Transparent World".
